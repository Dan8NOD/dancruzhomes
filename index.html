How a build runs

Production-builder process, single-builder finish.

Schedule pressure is the enemy of good homes. The discipline here is that every project follows the same four-step path — and that's why they finish on time.

01

Site & Program

Lot walk, code check, budget guardrails. 30 days. We say yes or no on the lot before you pay an architect.

02

Design & Permit

Floor plan locked to spec. Drawings to plan review in 60 days. No custom millwork theatre — only durable choices.

03

Build

One trade at a time, one floor a week. Subcontractors are paid by phase, not by draw — they finish faster.

04

Commissioning

ERV balance, HVAC commissioning, final punch list. White-glove walkthrough before keys hand over.
